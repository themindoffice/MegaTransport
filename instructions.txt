
ELEMENTS TO USE:

=========================================================
1. Content
=========================================================

Syntax:
---------------------------------------------------------

<content name="*name of iris object*">*content*</content>

---------------------------------------------------------
Example:
---------------------------------------------------------

<content name="page.intro-tekst[textarea]">
	Your content here
</content>

---------------------------------------------------------
result HTML:
---------------------------------------------------------

Your content here

---------------------------------------------------------
result PHP:
---------------------------------------------------------

{{page.intro-tekst[textarea]}}

=========================================================
2. Content (for inside attributes)
=========================================================

Syntax:
---------------------------------------------------------

content{*name of iris object*, *path to local file*}

---------------------------------------------------------
Example:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="content{page.intro-tekst[image], files/image.jpg}">

---------------------------------------------------------
result HTML:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="files/image.jpg">

---------------------------------------------------------
result PHP:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="{{page.intro-tekst[image]}}">

=========================================================
3. Slir
=========================================================

Syntax:
---------------------------------------------------------

slir{*properties*}

---------------------------------------------------------
Example:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="slir{w600}content{page.intro-tekst[image], files/image.jpg}">

---------------------------------------------------------
result HTML:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="files/image.jpg">

---------------------------------------------------------
result PHP:
---------------------------------------------------------

<img src="/slir/w600{{page.intro-tekst[image]}}">

=========================================================
4. View
=========================================================

Syntax:
---------------------------------------------------------

<view name="*name of view*"></view>

---------------------------------------------------------
Example:
---------------------------------------------------------

<view name="news">
	<article>News article</article>
	<article>News article</article>
	<article>News article</article>
</view>

---------------------------------------------------------
RESULT HTML:
---------------------------------------------------------

<article>News article</article>
<article>News article</article>
<article>News article</article>

---------------------------------------------------------
RESULT PHP:
---------------------------------------------------------

@views_news@

=========================================================
5. Include
=========================================================

Syntax:
---------------------------------------------------------

@include('*folder name*/*include name*', {
	*optional parameters*
})

---------------------------------------------------------
Example:
---------------------------------------------------------

@include('header/_header.php', {
	"image": "files/banner-1.jpg",
	"title": "Hello world"
})

---------------------------------------------------------
RESULT HTML:
---------------------------------------------------------

<header>Whatever html you put into the include</header>

---------------------------------------------------------
RESULT PHP:
---------------------------------------------------------

<?php include("includes/_header.php") ?>
